SensEH:

A software framework that allows developers to move back and forth between the power and speed of a simulated approach and the reality and accuracy of in-field experiments. SENSEH relies on COOJA/MSPSim for emulating the actual, deployment-ready code, and provides two modes of operation that allow the reuse of exactly the same code in real-world WSN deployments.

For further information, please forward to https://github.com/usmanraza/SensEH-Contiki.
